Fastify has always been about doing more with less. The new website is our
attempt to make that feeling tangible the moment you land on the page: a live
throughput gauge, a telemetry-inspired layout, and documentation that gets out
of your way.

## What changed

- **A signature hero** — a requests-per-second gauge that animates on load.
- **Faster docs** — statically generated, instantly searchable.
- **Clear paths** — guides, reference, ecosystem, and benchmarks, all one click away.

## Same power, new paint

Everything you relied on is still here: the plugin ecosystem, the benchmark
comparisons, the organizations using Fastify in production, and the team behind
it all. We simply gave it a home that moves as fast as the framework does.

Want to help shape it? The site is open source on
[GitHub](https://github.com/fastify/website).
